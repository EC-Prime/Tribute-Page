Walter White

"One of the most iconic characters in television history."

Here's a time line of Walter White:

  • Sept 7, 1958: Born to an only child; father dies of Huntington’s disease when Walt is 6.
  • 1980s: Graduates from Caltech, co-founds Gray Matter Technologies with Elliott Schwartz and girlfriend Gretchen.
  • 1980s: Abruptly leaves Gretchen and sells his share of Gray Matter for $5,000 due to personal insecurities, missing out on massive success.
  • 1985: Contributes to research awarded the Nobel Prize in Chemistry.
  • Pre-2008: Marries Skyler, has a son (Walter Jr.), and works as a high school chemistry teacher and part-time car wash employee.
  • Sept 7, 2008 (50th Birthday): Diagnosed with terminal lung cancer.
  • Oct 2008: Produces first batch of meth with former student Jesse Pinkman; kills Krazy-8, marking his first murders.
  • 2009: Adopts the alias "Heisenberg." Partners with Gustavo Fring, creating a vast distribution network.
  • 2009: Orchestrates the death of Gus Fring via Hector Salamanca.
  • 2009-2010: Operates as sole kingpin; murders Mike Ehrmantraut. DEA Agent Hank Schrader discovers Walt's identity.
  • 2010: Hank is killed by Neo-Nazis, to whom Walt sold out Jesse. Walt goes into hiding in New Hampshire.
  • Sept 7, 2010 (52nd Birthday): Returns to Albuquerque, rescues Jesse from the Neo-Nazis, kills Jack Welker’s gang using an automated machine gun, and dies from a gunshot wound in their lab.
